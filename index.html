Bringing vintage jazz to life

Golden Earrings Jazz Band plays 1920s and 1930s favorites by the likes of Josephine Baker, Mamie Smith, Connie Boswell, Fats Waller, Sophie Tucker, Edith Piaf, and Sidney Bechet.

From New Orleans standards to French chanson, we've got what you need to feel like you're living in another time â€” and we love to throw in a few country and Latin favorites as well.

The core of Golden Earrings Jazz Band is an engaging and versatile duo: Kevin "Muscle Mouth" Buster on saxophone, guitar, and foot percussion; and Robin Kallsen on vocals and upright bass.

Together, we create an authentic prohibition-era sound that you might expect from a much larger ensemble.

Want an even bigger sound? We can add more musicians!

We regularly work with:

Swing Vocalist, Ukuleleist, Pianist, Cornetist, and Composer Casey MacGill

Country Blues Guitarist and Singer-Songwriter Steve Itterly
