Travel back in musical history with the Golden Earrings Jazz Band!

Step into the 1920s, '30s and '40s by listening (and dancing!) to a duo of multi-instrumentalists who truly live and breathe that era's music. Golden Earrings Jazz Band plays favorites by the likes of Josephine Baker, Mamie Smith, Connie Boswell, Fats Waller, Sophie Tucker, Edith Piaf, and Sidney Bechet. From New Orleans standards to French chanson, we've got what you need to feel like you're living in another time â€” and we love to throw in a few country and Latin favorites as well.

The core of Golden Earrings Jazz Band is an engaging and versatile duo: Kevin "Muscle Mouth" Buster on saxophone, guitar, and foot percussion; and Robin Kallsen on vocals and upright bass. Together, they create an authentic sound that one would expect from a much larger ensemble.

Want an even bigger sound? We can add more musicians!
